Economic policies and regulations play a significant role in shaping global trade dynamics. Governments must balance protectionism and free trade to foster economic growth. Collaborative efforts, such as trade agreements, are essential in promoting stability and prosperity in the global economy.
The global economy is interconnected, with trade and investment flowing across borders. International markets offer opportunities for businesses to expand and reach new customers. However, economic interdependence also means that a crisis in one region can have ripple effects worldwide.
